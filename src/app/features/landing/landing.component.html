Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about how ReviewBoost works? We have answers.

How does the review routing work?

When customers scan your QR code, they are directed to a clean mobile-friendly page listing all your active review channels. Clicking any platform link records the event and redirects the customer instantly to complete their review.

Can I customize my QR code's destination URL?

Yes! By upgrading to the Growth or Pro plans, you can customize your QR code slug (e.g. /r/mybusinessname). You can also add and disable review links in your dashboard instantly.

Do you charge any setup fees?

No. There are no setup fees, hidden costs, or long-term contracts. You can start with our Free Starter plan to test the product and scale as your traffic grows.
